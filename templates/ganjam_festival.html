Festivals of Ganjam

Danda Nacha / Nata

A ritual dance drama dedicated to Goddess Kali and Lord Shiva, performed in Chaitra (March-April). Dancers (‘danduas’) undergo austerities and perform barefoot with mud, water, and fire rituals. Its origins date back to the 8th–9th centuries.

Kandhei Jatra

An age-old festival featuring traditional wooden dolls, offered to Budhi Thakurani temple in Berhampur. The festival reflects Ganjam’s folk traditions.

Bharata Lila

A performing theatre based on the Mahabharata, also called Duari Nata. It highlights Arjuna & Subhadra’s romance, blending music, acting, and devotion.
